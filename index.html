Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important way of communication among chameleons.
  • Chameleons wrap their tails around trees branches to stick to them.

Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are known for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • They have an exellent hearing ability.

Camel

  • Camel's ears are furry.
  • Camels are known as the "ships of the desert".
  • Camels can live upto 15 DAYS without water.

Cicada

  • There are around 3,000 cicada species.
  • Cicadas can be black, brown or green and can have red, white or blue eyes.
  • Their wings are transparent and can seem rainbow-hued when held up to a light source.

Panda

  • Their eyes are different from other bears.
  • Some people have pandas as pets.
